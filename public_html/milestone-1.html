Milestone 1

Purpose,Audience & Goal

Purpose

:

The purpose of my Professional Web Portfolio is to establish a professional online presence that highlights my skills in full-stack web development and design. It will serve as a digital showcase for my work, emphasizing my technical abilities and creative approach to front-end and user experience design. This site aims to communicate my value to potential employers and collaborators within the IT and web development fields.

Audience

:

This site is designed for a professional audience, primarily targeting hiring managers, recruiters, startup founders, and industry peers within the tech and design communities. It is also intended for potential freelance clients who are seeking innovative, well-crafted web solutions for their projects.

Goal

:

The main goal of this site is to generate interest in my work, leading to employment or freelance project opportunities in the IT and web development field. I aim to have users reach out for job or collaboration inquiries through a contact form and/or connected social media profiles. A secondary goal is to establish a professional reputation and grow my network within the industry.

Persona

Name

: Renay Moya

Age

: 52

Profession

:

Senior Vice President of the New Mexico Technology Division at Robert Half Talent Solutions

Technology

:

Renay utilizes the latest Apple devices to efficiently manage her role as Senior Vice President at Robert Half. Mac Power User: Renay works on a 2024 Mac Pro with dual 32” Pro Display XDR monitors in the office, and a 2024 MacBook Pro for remote work. Mobile Connectivity: She stays connected with an iPhone 15 Pro Max and 5G, ensuring she can communicate with clients and candidates anytime. Recruitment Tools: Renay leverages top ATS platforms and market analytics tools to streamline hiring processes and stay informed on industry trends. Communication: She frequently uses LinkedIn, Zoom, and Microsoft Teams to network, conduct interviews, and stay in touch with her professional community. Her cutting-edge tech setup ensures seamless productivity and connectivity.

Background and Expertise

:

With over 20 years of experience in IT staffing and consulting, Renay specializes in connecting IT professionals and employers, helping to shape New Mexico’s tech landscape. As a liaison between companies and candidates, he has worked extensively with thousands of hiring managers and IT professionals to address specific hiring needs in areas such as Helpdesk, Desktop Support, Networking, Database Management, Applications Development, Software/Web Development, Quality Assurance, Technical Writing, Project Management, and Healthcare IT. Renay is known for his deep understanding of the local IT market and is a trusted advisor in helping employers position themselves as top-choice workplaces.

Behaviors and Attitudes

:

Renay is extremely efficient, results-driven, and values clear, concise information when reviewing potential candidates. With a full schedule and high client demands, she prioritizes portfolios and resumes that are well-organized and tailored to the job market. Renay seeks candidates who present both technical qualifications and a strong cultural fit for the companies she represents. As a leader in staffing, she also focuses on guiding employers to create environments that attract top talent. She is a strong advocate for professionalism and transparency and expects the same from candidates.

Frustrations

:

High Volume of Candidates: Renay frequently reviews large numbers of candidates, which makes it frustrating when portfolios are disorganized or lacking relevant information. Cultural Fit: While many candidates possess the required technical skills, Renay struggles to find individuals who also demonstrate the interpersonal and professional attributes that align with her clients' company cultures. Limited Time: Renay often needs to fill positions quickly, meaning she does not have time for portfolios that are difficult to navigate or lacking essential details. Efficiency is critical to her decision-making process.

Goals

Identify Qualified Candidates Quickly: Renay’s main goal when visiting a portfolio is to quickly assess if the candidate’s skills, experience, and personality align with the job openings she’s recruiting for. She wants to make this assessment within 3 minutes. Evaluate Cultural Fit: In addition to technical qualifications, Renay looks for signs that the candidate would integrate well into a company’s culture. She values candidates who can highlight their team collaboration, leadership, and adaptability. Facilitate Immediate Contact: Renay aims to connect with candidates who are a good fit, so a clear contact form or social media links are essential for fast communication.

Additional Information Needed

:

Relevant Skill Showcase: Renay needs a well-structured portfolio that clearly presents the candidate’s skills, experience, and projects related to her focus areas in IT. Certifications or relevant coursework in areas like software development or database management are valuable additions. Professionalism and Organization: A clean, well-organized layout that showcases experience without excessive clutter. She prefers portfolios that highlight both technical expertise and a candidate's professionalism. Quick Access to Contact Info: Renay prefers portfolios that make it easy to find contact information (email, LinkedIn, etc.) so she can initiate communication quickly if interested. Insight into Cultural Fit: Renay appreciates testimonials, team project details, or any evidence of a candidate’s ability to work well with others, which can help her determine cultural fit.

User Story

:

As Senior Vice President in IT staffing, Renay needs to quickly assess whether a candidate’s portfolio demonstrates the technical skills, professionalism, and cultural fit required for the roles she is filling. She doesn’t have much time, so the portfolio must be easy to navigate and provide a clear, concise showcase of the candidate's qualifications, allowing her to decide if she should pursue further contact.
